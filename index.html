Dr. Alex Chen

Research Fellow in Computational Linguistics, FutureTech Institute

Welcome! I am a researcher specializing in the intersection of large language models (LLMs), cognitive science, and natural language understanding. My work focuses on developing explainable AI systems for text generation and evaluating the structural biases in conversational agents. I am currently leading the 'Cognitive Semantics Project' at the FutureTech Institute, exploring how abstract concepts are encoded in neural networks.

Google Scholar GitHub Email

Research Areas

Explainable AI (XAI)

Developing post-hoc interpretation methods and intrinsically explainable architectures for complex deep learning models, particularly in NLP tasks.

Cognitive Semantics

Investigating the neuro-symbolic representation of abstract concepts, emotion, and metaphor within neural language models.

Conversational Agents

Focus on dialogue systems, ethical AI, and mitigating bias in large-scale human-computer interaction contexts.
